# Parkly — Product Summary

Parkly is a smart parking intelligence platform by ZEUS Technologies. It operates as a two-sided marketplace connecting drivers who need parking with hosts (property owners) who have unused spaces.

## Core Concept

"Airbnb for parking" — any unused parking space becomes discoverable, bookable, and monetizable through the platform.

## Primary Users

- **Drivers**: Find, reserve, navigate to, and pay for parking digitally.
- **Hosts**: List spaces, set availability/pricing, earn passive income.
- **Businesses**: Monetize parking, reduce congestion, track occupancy.
- **Government**: Manage city parking, analytics, demand forecasting, violation tracking.

## Key Differentiators

- AI-powered availability prediction (not just current status)
- Dynamic pricing based on demand
- Full marketplace with trust/verification layer
- Government and enterprise analytics dashboards
- Designed for scale: single city → national → global

## Product Surfaces

- Driver mobile app (search, book, navigate, pay)
- Host dashboard (listings, calendar, revenue, payouts)
- Admin dashboard (users, fraud, disputes, monitoring)
- Government dashboard (heatmaps, forecasting, reports)

## Current Phase

Phase 1 — MVP: Manual onboarding, one city, rule-based AI, basic marketplace.
